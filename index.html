Industry Night

THE GOOD FOUNDATION INC. THEATRE

Join us for a showcase featuring the work of our IDP3 and UED students, alongside Spotlight presentations highlighting the The Brothers in Arms project.

Experience the talent and dedication of our students as they proudly present their portfolios, showcasing their passion and skills across various disciplines.

After the showcase and viewing of portfolios, we’ll continue the celebration across the street at the Chef’s Table. Enjoy appetizers and a cash bar while mingling with fellow attendees. It’s the perfect opportunity to network, reconnect with old friends, and forge new connections.

INTERACTIVE MEDIA DESIGN & USER EXPERIENCE DESIGN PROGRAM

Embark on a creative journey at Fanshawe College with our combined Interactive Media Design and User Experience Design programs! Here, you’ll master the art of crafting captivating digital experiences while honing your skills in user-centered design. Engage in hands-on projects that allow you to create stunning websites, immersive games, and intuitive interfaces, all in collaboration with industry professionals. As you build a standout portfolio, you’ll be prepared to thrive in the dynamic world of interactive media. Join us at Fanshawe, and transform your passion for design into impactful solutions that inspire and connect with users!
